Transportation in Taniti

Getting to Taniti

Air Transportation

Most visitors arrive by air, through our small airport that accommodates small jets and propeller planes. An expansion is underway to handle larger jets.

Cruise Ships

Some visitors arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Getting Around Taniti

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis

Taxis are available in Taniti City for convenient transportation.

Rental Cars

Rental cars can be rented from a local agency near the airport.

Bikes

Bikes and helmets are available for rent from several vendors. Helmets are required by law.

Walking

Many tourists stay in Merriton Landing, which is easy to explore on foot. Taniti City is flat and very walkable.
