Welcome to Taniti

About the Island

Taniti is a stunning tropical island in the Pacific. Its diverse terrain includes sandy and rocky beaches, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior with an active volcano. Home to about 20,000 indigenous people, Taniti's economy was traditionally based on fishing and agriculture. Recently, tourism has flourished, offering visitors a range of amenities such as diverse restaurants, various lodging options, and numerous recreational activities,

Top Attractions

Enjoy the beautiful beaches, explore the rainforests, or visit the active volcano. In Taniti City, marvel at native architecture and relax on white sandy beaches around Yellow Leaf Bay. Merriton Landing, a developing area, offers a local history museum, fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining, a microbrewery, dance club, movie theater, helicopter rides, arcade, art galleries, and bowling. A nine-hole golf course will soon be available.
